About Me

Hello! I'm a Visual Communication and Design Graduate from Eastern Washington University, as well as the former Cofounder and Chief Technical Officer for Green Filter Guys. I have a passion for design in all forms, and I take pride in my ability to rapidly create visual representations for ideas in all forms. Between freelancing, volunteer work and general passion projects, you can find my work scattered far and wide across the internet. This website is intended a small look into some of the work I've done, and was coded using chatgpt. Thank you for visiting!
