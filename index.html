About Me

Hello! I'm Ryan Van Meter, a Visual Communication and Design graduate from Eastern Washington University and the former Cofounder and Chief Technical Officer for Green Filter Guys. I have a passion for design in all its forms, and I take pride in my ability to rapidly create visual representations of ideas. Between freelancing, volunteer work, and general passion projects, you can find my work scattered across the internet. This website offers a small glimpse into some of the work I've done, and it was coded with the help of ChatGPT. Outside of design, I am a new father, a devoted husband, and a hobbyist content creator. I enjoy stand-up comedy and 3D printing, and I value my family above all else. Thank you for visiting!
